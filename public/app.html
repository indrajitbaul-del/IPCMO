Cutting allowance chart — industry standard

How cutting allowance is calculated:
Total allowance = Kerf (cutting blade width) + Facing/bevel prep loss
Example: 4" CS pipe, plasma cut = 4mm kerf + 3mm bevel face = 7mm total
Default 5mm used when not specified. Bevel allowance applies when pipe ends require bevelling for welding (butt weld prep per ASME B16.25).
